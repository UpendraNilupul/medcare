Excellence in Healthcare Since 1985

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Medcare Hospital has grown from a small community clinic to one of Sri Lanka's most trusted healthcare institutions with 38+ years of excellence.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional healthcare services combining medical excellence with compassionate care for every patient, ensuring the highest standards of treatment and support.

Our Vision

To be Sri Lanka's leading healthcare provider, recognized for innovation, quality, and patient-centered care that transforms lives and communities.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings as a community clinic, we've evolved into a comprehensive healthcare facility serving over 1 million patients with dedication and excellence.
